Xingyue Huo is a biostatistician at Mount Sinai Hospital &. He received his Ph.D. in 2018 from the University of California, Davis, as a part of the VIDI lab under Dr. Kwan-Liu Ma. His research spans the areas of data visualization, human-computer interaction, and data science. At ASU, he heads the Sonoran Visualization Laboratory.
