    Perturbations in neural networks
    Hashmi, R.
    Pending submission 2021
    Abs HTML PDF

    In this paper, we propose a novel way to reduce generalization error in neural networks. A standard neural network works by propagating input data through its hidden layers and improving their predictions through gradient descent on the errors. However, with limited training data and huge network size, this typically leads to over-fitting the input data. To counter this effect, we propagate a perturbation through the network along with training data
