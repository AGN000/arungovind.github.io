Hi there! I’m Shray, the creator of this website. Thanks for stumbling here. It is my sincerest hope that your experience is net-positive and you discover something that aids you, even in the most miniscule manner. As the description states, small changes can have dramatic effects over time.

A little about me: At university (Michigan), I immersed myself in a latticework of disciplines comprised of political economy, business, and economics (A.B & BBA). In particular, my academic background has encouraged me to pose somewhat non-parochial questions to profound matters of our time, while assiduously pondering about how to frame the complicated world around me in the face of radical uncertainty and ever-evolving challenges. In general, university unveiled numerous possibilities and occasionally, it seemed as if I had opened an anti-pandora’s box.

My takeaway will be one remembering the love, knowledge, and memories that I shared with people who are on their own intrepid journeys. One chapter closes and the next must begin; quite the natural cycle. Moreover, I’ve dabbled in entrepreneurial ventures, interned at a now-defunct investment company, built a private market-focused club after reading a book (yes, this is true), and worked in a product role at a student-run tech business. My interests at large span from financial markets to academia to the arts. If you’re interested in my experiences, here’s my linkedin. If you would like to connect, please send me an email and I’ll do my best to respond. Also, I write at social insights, a media outlet aimed at providing subscribers with atomic bytes from the world’s most fascinating people and mediums. Thanks for reading - I wish you nothing but love.
